Cheese Chasers (1951) *** (out of 4) The final of six films in the Hubie and Bertie series has the two miceeating so much cheese that it actually makes them never want to have itagain. So, with that in mind, they decide to end their lives byoffering themselves up to Claude the Cat but he can't understand whythey're doing it so he refuses to eat them. Soon Claude is wanting thelocal bulldog to eat him but he too refuses. This is a fairly cute andentertaining short that manages to have enough charm and laughs to keepit going. The entire situation of someone wanting the higher up to eatthem is pretty funny and it makes for some solid entertaining. Therearen't any major laughs but there are enough here to make this worthviewing.
